Origin

Tabitha Sawyer began her life adopted by a rural family known as 'the Walkers', given to a nice couple in Florida. Late in life, her brother — a man named Void Walker known to be possessed by an otherworldly entity called 'the Void' — passed. A piece of this being passed to her, causing a psychotic break. Through this break she began to call herself 'Stabitha' and speak of a desire to maim or kill her foes, referencing a history of being a 'creature from the Void.' Whatever that means.
