The Shows

Programming
Weekly Live Broadcast
WICKED Live
The flagship live event of Pure Amusement Wrestling. Broadcast from inside the Pure Amusement Park in Purity, Louisiana. Four episodes streamed, all available in the WrestleFlix archive. WICKED Live set the tone — strange, electric, and unlike anything else in the game.
DVD Taping Series
Addiction
The tape-exclusive companion to WICKED Live. Four issues of the Addiction DVD taping series were released, each featuring content that didn't make it to broadcast. Collectable, raw, and completely essential for the true PAW completionist.
📻
Radio Program · 8 Episodes
The PAW Report
A radio show hosted by Press and Lady Munin. Eight episodes of commentary, rumors, and coverage from inside the PAW universe. The PAW Report ran alongside WICKED Live and Addiction, offering an in-universe audio experience unlike anything else in e-fed history.
