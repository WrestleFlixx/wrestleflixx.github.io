About This Series

inVader chronicles the life of a cosmic entity of ancient and terrible power as it adjusts to human existence, professional wrestling, and the logistical reality of needing a body that functions. The series balances genuine sci-fi mythology — the Vitaeum Council, the Great Filter, the Camlann Pact — with the deeply grounded mundanity of sharing a bedroom with a roommate who needs to be reminded not to schedule matches during regenerative cocoon time.

It is, at its core, a show about two people sharing a body, one of whom conquered galaxies and one of whom has a WrestleFlixx deal she definitely earned and did not receive via cosmic occupation. The prune juice is real. The threat is also real.
