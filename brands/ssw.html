The Promotion
SSW × CCW Universe
Satan-Slanted Wrestling and Christ-Centered Wrestling are two halves of the same machine. They share a roster, share storylines, and run the CCW/SSW 100 Year Anniversary as a combined two-night event. All championships from both promotions are defended across both nights. Brennan Devlin runs both. Nobody asks why.
The Talent
SSW draws from the harder, more confrontational corners of the Devlin universe's talent pool. Silas Romero, Stabitha, JD Driftwood, Hatchet Gully, and Harper Lee all have history here. The tone is rawer than CCW. The production quality is identical because it's the same company.
About Satan-Slanted Wrestling

SSW is Brennan Devlin's second wrestling promotion, operating in tandem with CCW as a combined brand. The relationship between the two promotions is best described by the fact that both exist, both function, and nobody has successfully demanded an explanation for why a man named Brennan Devlin owns both Christ-Centered Wrestling and Satan-Slanted Wrestling simultaneously.

The 100 Year CCW/SSW Anniversary is the flagship joint event — two nights, all titles, every significant storyline from both promotions reaching a conclusion simultaneously.
