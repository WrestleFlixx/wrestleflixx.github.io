The Promotion
Owner / Operator
Brennan Devlin runs CCW alongside Satan-Slanted Wrestling as a combined promotional universe. He is simultaneously a performer, a champion in multiple companies, and the person signing the paychecks. He does not always remember which of his children's names are which.
CCW × SSW Universe
Christ-Centered Wrestling and Satan-Slanted Wrestling operate as complementary promotions sharing talent, storylines, and joint events. The 100 Year CCW Anniversary is a two-night crossover event. Both nights feature championship matches from both promotions.
Notable Debuts
The Red Lock Legion (Scarlett, Flynn, and Corsen Howell) debuted in CCW and won their first match. As Brennan himself noted: somebody is gunning for their heads now. Scarlett is optimistic. Flynn is Flynn.

About Christ-Centered Wrestling

CCW is Brennan Devlin's wrestling promotion and part of his growing empire of championships and companies. As he described it to Scarlett Howell on her talk show: he wants more gold, more success, more everything — for himself and for everyone he thinks is the future of the business. CCW is one vehicle for that.

The tone is less pious than the name implies. Brennan Devlin runs it. This should set expectations appropriately.
