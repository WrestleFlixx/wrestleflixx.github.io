The Three Divisions

Structure
Paramount
Premier Division
The crown jewel of the AWE brand. The Paramount Division housed the most decorated competitors — those competing for the AWE Championship itself.
Resilience
Rising Division
Built for competitors who earned their place through persistence. The Resilience Division produced some of AWE's most memorable underdog stories.
Dynamic
High-Velocity Division
Fast. Innovative. Unpredictable. The Dynamic Division was where high-flyers and rule-breakers made their names before ascending to Paramount.
