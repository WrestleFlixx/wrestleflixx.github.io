Biography

Victoria Ann Gable wasn't supposed to be a wrestler. Her budding modeling career brought her to the sport — and what started as a curiosity became an obsession. Under the guidance and mentorship of Shawn Clarke, V.A.G. was forged in training gyms, taking her early beatings and coming back stronger each time. Her repertoire has evolved rapidly: a versatile striker, high flyer, and budding submission technician. She'll find any advantage. She'll use it. She doesn't apologize for being good at this.
