Psychological Profile

Profoundly pragmatic, utterly alien in her moral compass. She does not see "good" or "evil" but "alignment" and "obstruction." Her loyalty is to the prophecy of Oceiros's bloodline and its perpetuation. She views WrestleFlix not as an employer, but as a powerful new "tribe" to be manipulated for her ends.

She is patient, calculating, and considers modern technology a fascinating, crude form of magic. Her current presentation is strikingly athletic with long, dark hair often adorned with feathers or small carved bones that seem to shift when not directly observed. Her eyes appear ancient and perceive more than what is visually present.

Asset Relations notes: Do not offer her artifacts of significant age. Do not give her access to the ring canvas without the synthesized polymer lining. Do not allow eye contact with opponents during Oceiros's finisher sequences.
