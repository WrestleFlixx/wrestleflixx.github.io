Biography

Hatchet Gully grew up all around Pennsylvania but JD Driftwood grew up in Key West, Florida — the kind of place that makes men either soft or extremely mean. JD went the second route. Trained by his father Cutter Driftwood in the old-school tradition, he learned to wrestle the way wrestlers used to: getting stretched, slammed, and brutalized from a young age, all in the name of paying his dues.

His career has taken him through Evolution, CULT, NVR, the NAWL, and eventually WrestleMecca where he captured the Apocalypse Championship — the belt awarded to the most destructive, most battle-worn fighters to ever do it. An ill-fated tour of Japan ended in a prison stay. He lost his left eye while inside. He doesn't talk about how. He rebuilt, kept wrestling, kept winning.

He makes sure his moves hurt. Throwing his weight full in, stretching back just that inch more. He wants you to feel what he's doing to you. He's a real shooter, JD. That's not a metaphor.
