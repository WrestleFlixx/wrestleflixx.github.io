The Mystery

It's very difficult to ascertain the truth about Kassandrah's background. She first appeared as a fortune teller and tarot reader on the midway at the Pure Amusement park in Purity, Louisiana in 2014, where she would infrequently appear as a guest star in Pure Amusement Wrestling segments. From there she went on to be one of a trio of owners and operators of the Alpha Wrestling Empire, and later the first iteration of Fight 2 Win in 2017. Kassandrah has appeared on several underground fighting videos, but nothing in any mainstream. Her date of birth — February 30, 1930 — is a date that does not exist on any calendar. No one has asked her about this directly. Anyone who has considered doing so found the thought leaving their mind before they could act on it.
