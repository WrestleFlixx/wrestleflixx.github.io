Biography

Erica Bragg began as an agent and consort for Dare Clemmens, but her natural talent and charisma soon propelled her into the wrestling ring. She rose fast — the kind of rise that draws attention and enemies in equal measure. It ended badly. Assault and possession of narcotics charges. Five years in prison.

Now she's back. Freshly released. In the best shape of her life. She wants to shed the bad girl image. Whether the wrestling world will let her do that on her own terms remains to be seen. She knows what she's carrying. She's carrying it anyway.
