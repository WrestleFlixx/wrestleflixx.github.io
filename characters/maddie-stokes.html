Personality

Maddie Elizabeth Stokes is charismatic and determined with a strong sense of independence and a remarkable ability to connect with fans of any stripe. She is fiercely passionate about wrestling, dedicated to carving her own path in the industry. Despite her estranged relationship with her father Brad Stokes, she is driven by a desire to establish her own identity apart from the Stokes name, while still honouring his legacy.

Maddie is known for her resilience and ability to bounce back from adversity. Her opportunistic mindset and strategic thinking make her a formidable competitor. However, her fiery and passionate nature can sometimes lead to overly melodramatic or emotional reactions — making her susceptible to mind games. Her overconfidence and risk-taking tendencies are double-edged swords, leading to both spectacular victories and costly mistakes.
