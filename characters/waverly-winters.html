Biography

Waverly Winters comes from a family long embedded in the music and entertainment industries of Pigeon Forge, Tennessee. Despite showing no interest in either endeavor herself, her mother Claudia found an opportunity to capitalize on Waverly's exceptional athletic ability — first entering her in fitness competitions, which led to meeting a local wrestling promoter who saw something in her.

She reluctantly took the promoter's advice and began training. She kept her day job as a physical trainer. She still has it. When it's showtime, she fights off the jitters to channel her version of what she privately calls "a country Emma Frost." She keeps to herself for the most part. She may seem intimidating to some — until you strike up a conversation with her. Then she is fine. She just doesn't initiate.

Claudia Winters, her mother and manager — a force in an ice blue skirt suit — is far more talkative. Someone has to be.
