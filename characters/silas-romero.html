Gimmick — Generic American Strong Style Guy

Silas doesn't have a difficult gimmick to explain. He really doesn't have one. In the early years of his career, he tried to put on a fake front — tried to be someone he wasn't. He had moderate success on the indies but never found his footing when he jumped to bigger promotions. It wasn't until he started incorporating more of his real personality that things clicked. The first glimpse: calling himself the "Hipster Hercules," because he finally embraced that he genuinely was a hipster. Now? His in-ring persona and his real-life persona have merged into one. Generic Strong Style Guy. He no longer hides his nerdy side. He is, per his own assessment, everything he says he is.
