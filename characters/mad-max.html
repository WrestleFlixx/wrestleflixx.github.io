Biography

Maxine Maddox, known as "Mad Max," seemingly appeared out of nowhere, claiming to be from the future. With a genius-level IQ and an uncanny ability to master wrestling techniques, she quickly rose to prominence. Her mysterious background and strategic approach to matches have made her a formidable opponent. Operating in a morally grey area, Mad Max is always looking for the quickest way to victory, regardless of the methods used.

When 5 Lakes Wrestling's owner Frank James was asked about her signing, he noted: "Mad Max is going to be a star. Or so she tells me, anyhow. I said we'll see and she explained she already has, so make of that what you will."
