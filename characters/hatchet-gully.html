Biography

Hatchet Gully grew up all around Pennsylvania, never really fitting in anywhere. He adjusted and adapted to whatever life brought him, and felt at home in the streets as well as in the mountains. His status as an outcast made the life of a Juggalo appealing — he met A$hley Quid at a Gathering event and from then on strove to make life better for the both of them.

They tried to do this legitimately through wrestling and somewhat illegally through whatever hustle they could get away with. This led to troubles with the law — particularly an ill-fated tour of Japan where he was sent to prison. He lost his left eye while inside. He doesn't talk about how. Forced to rebuild, they put their focus more into wrestling.

Gully is a dreamer underneath the menace. A glory hunter. He's already lost everything twice and built it back up both times. Any threat to what he's built is met with threats and violence. He never really grew out of the high school mentality, even after traveling the world. His comfort zone is his hometown. He will fight his way to the top on his own special terms, whether you like it or not.
