Executive Producer · Director · Manager · Recurring Problem
Francis Ford Cuppola
The legendary but baffling auteur whose resume includes Barackula, the Rhombus, and the Ani-Rhombus. He does not understand mimes. He does not understand that he does not understand them. He considers himself their manager, their visionary, and their greatest advocate. Rodney P considers him a logistical catastrophe. The mimes have no recorded opinion. Cuppola has not stopped talking long enough to notice.
About This Series

The French Mime Assassins chronicles the debut season of Comme Çi and Comme Ça — professional wrestlers from France who happen to be practicing mimes — in their journey through the New Japan Fighting Championship Tag-Team Tournament and beyond into Pure Amusement Wrestling. Captured through the lens of their reluctant assistant Rodney P and the unhinged film producer Francis Ford Cuppola, the series blends observational comedy with genuine wrestling drama, producing a show that has never existed before and will likely never be repeated.

The mimes do not speak. The show does not suffer for it. Quite the opposite.
