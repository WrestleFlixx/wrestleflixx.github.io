About AWE Massacre

AWE Massacre is the weekly broadcast of the Alpha Wrestling Empire — a Boston-based wrestling promotion backed by the mysterious Mr. Smith and operated by one of the most chaotic management trios in wrestling history: Francis Ford Cuppola (film director, syrup enthusiast), Kassandrah (fortune teller, eventual COO), and Thirteen (the organized one). Together and separately they search for a missing championship, manage an alien-obsessed boss, and somehow put on a wrestling show every two weeks.

The French Mime Assassins appear across multiple episodes as AWE guest talent. HAL 9000 is a studio AI who gets decommissioned in episode four. Nate Hollis hosts a show within the show until that also gets cancelled. Brad Stokes is the announcer and he's having a great time.
