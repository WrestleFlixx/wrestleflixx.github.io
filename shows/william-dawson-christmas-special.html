The Three Events
🏃
The Mistletoe Run
🏆 Prize: All-Expenses Paid Trip to Courchevel, France (New Year's)
Teams of two. You cannot drop the mistletoe sprig or you're disqualified. Race through the obstacle course. Reach your partner at the other end. Kiss. Win. Simple. Romantic. Competitive.
🎤
The Christmas Pageant
🏆 Prize: Golden Trophy Filled With Money (Split if group)
Solo or group performance. Sing, dance, or both. William and his family judge. They hold up scorecards. Thot Experiment (@ThotExperimentt) entered. The BBC entered with Skylar Wright Virtue. This will be something.
🍪
The Cookie Jar
🏆 Prize: Trip to Switzerland + Spending Money (Chocolate Capital)
William cannot drink his traditional Three Wise Men shot this year. But he can eat cookies. The winner is judged on taste AND presentation. Christmas-themed cookies score best. William alone judges.

About The Special

William Dawson's Christmas Special is a one-night holiday event hosted by PAW veteran William Dawson, who following heart surgery has committed to using his platform positively. He is using the prize money from his cage match victory over Carlo to fund the event's prizes entirely out of his own pocket.

He is dressed as Santa Claus for the entire evening. His family is present. He welcomes everyone — whether you love or hate him — in the spirit of the holidays. This is genuine. His heart has literally been opened. His eyes have also been opened. He would like to give back, and he has done so by giving two trips, a trophy of money, and a great deal of Christmas spirit to professional wrestlers.
