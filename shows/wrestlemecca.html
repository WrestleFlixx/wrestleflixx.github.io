The Lore
The Wish Mechanism
At WrestleMecca, the event's central prize is not only a championship — it is a wish. Granted to the winner under specific conditions, a wish at WrestleMecca has the power to alter the shape of the WrestleFlix universe. The mechanism is not fully understood. The consequences always are, eventually.
Brad Stokes — GM
WrestleMecca's General Manager and biggest Zack Fantana fan. Commentator. Father of Maddie Stokes, with whom he has a complicated relationship. He awarded Maddie the Complete Championship through his GM authority. She is aware. They haven't spoken clearly about it.
Devlin's Legacy
Brennan Devlin's wish at WrestleMecca rewrote reality. He built F2W City — a dystopian future version of the Fight 2 Win promotion's environment. He watches his creation from the DevlinSaka Tower as a hologram, having made his wish and now living with the architecture of what it built.
About WrestleMecca

WrestleMecca is the premier PPV event in the WrestleFlix universe — the annual convergence where stakes are highest, the roster is largest, and the outcome can literally rewrite the rules of existence. The 32-person tournament for the WrestleMecca Apocalypse Championship is the centerpiece, with the wish mechanism reserved for moments that change everything.

General Manager Brad Stokes oversees the event with a combination of genuine passion for wrestling, unshakeable loyalty to Zack Fantana, and a complicated parental dynamic with his daughter Maddie, who is now a champion because of a decision he made that she didn't ask for.
