About Valor Pro Blitz

Valor Pro Wrestling's Blitz series is the promotion's flagship broadcast — an international touring event that rotates between Gran Canaria, Rome, Munich, and other European venues. Overseen by GM Vanessa Byrne, the series is marked by her controversial decision to designate Cosmo Cooper and Vannah White as her "Champions of Choice" from their very first appearance, bypassing the veterans who had been building toward those opportunities.

Brodie is the show's breakout character — a methodical, cryptic striker who delivers parking lot monologues while painting murals, and who turns every backstage confrontation into a power statement. The Othello Brothers (Kenneth and Laurence) call every event with chemistry and competence.
