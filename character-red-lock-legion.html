Biography

Scarlett Howell raised the twins Flynn and Corsen with one goal: success. Well disciplined, always taught to be proper. Wrestling was a family passion from a young age, and the twins were set up with training from childhood. They have had few challenges in life. The strict nature of their upbringing has, however, created fault lines. Corsen has become something of a rebel, frequently trying to annoy her parents. Flynn's upbringing has bred a superiority complex. Together — as a rotating trio where any two compete at any given time — they have entered wrestling with expectations that will likely not survive contact with the real world. But they haven't lost badly enough yet to know that.

They also host the "Howell Are You Doing?" podcast, through which they've interviewed Brennan Devlin, Silas Romero, Evie Locke, Coco Lazlo, and others from the wrestling world.
