About This Series

Howell Are You Doing? is a professional wrestling talk show and interview podcast hosted by Scarlett Howell from the Howell Studios. Each episode features one or more guests from across the wrestling landscape — champions, rivals, promoters, and everything in between — seated in the signature red chairs across from Scarlett for an hour of genuine conversation, mild tension, and sponsored wine.

The show is notable for its authenticity. Guests speak candidly. Feuds simmer under professional surfaces. Scarlett does her best Lois Lane and sometimes pulls it off brilliantly. Seven Oaks Winery has not regretted their sponsorship once, except for the Coco Lazlo episode.
