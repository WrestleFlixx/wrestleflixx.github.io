About This Series

Death Graps Wrestling is where the WrestleFlix universe gets its hands dirty. No commentary table is decorative. No light tube is for aesthetics. The promotion draws its talent from every corner of the roster — Silas Romero, JD Driftwood, Hatchet Gully, Cosmo Cooper, inVader — and puts them in matches where the goal is simple: survive, and ideally win.

The Death Graps Classic is the crown jewel of the promotion's calendar. Championships mean something here not because of prestige, but because of the price paid to hold them. Zack Fantana and American Tommy know this better than anyone.
